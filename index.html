About Us

The most trusted private primary school in Shoghi, shaping young minds for decades.

Focused on academic excellence, personal growth, and holistic development, we offer strong foundation to children.

We follow a CBSE-aligned curriculum while being recognized by the HP State Board. This ensures that our students can seamlessly transition to CBSE-affiliated schools after completing their primary education without facing learning gaps.

A lot of alumni students of Shoghi Public School have gone to Dayanand Public School,  DAV Public School,  St. Edward's School,  Chapslee School and other prestigious schools in Shimla and other cities.
